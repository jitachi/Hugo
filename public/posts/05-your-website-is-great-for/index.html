Ugh, my website.

I find it hard to work on my website. Treating it as a chore or part of my work (basically PR) makes it complicated for it to evolve. For that reason, I decided to list a number of reasons, excuses, and justifications to make writing feel more trivial.

  • Collecting thoughts you don’t want to hold anymore but don’t want to forget
  • Tell that story you think is funny or interesting
  • Write notes to yourself
  • Let people know you through the things you do every day. How you do anything is how you do everything.
  • Document progress. However it looks.
  • See how it looks “outside your head”
  • Telling the world what you like and dislike
  • Show your work.
  • Write your story.
  • Showing how you don’t care that much and that’s totally fine.
  • Put silly drawings up.
  • Share deep thoughts.
